Birds and Dinosaurs

Similarities

  • Both birds and dinosaurs are part of the archosaur group.
  • They share skeletal features, such as hollow bones and a wishbone.
  • Both groups lay eggs.

Differences

  • Birds have evolved for flight, with adaptations like feathers and a lightweight skeleton.
  • Dinosaurs include extinct species that were primarily terrestrial, while birds are a modern group with diverse habitats.
  • Modern birds have a more advanced respiratory system compared to dinosaurs.
